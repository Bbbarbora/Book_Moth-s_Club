Book of the Month

How to Stop Worrying and Start Living

Dale Carnegie

Tired of turning minor problems into apocalyptic disasters? Dale Carnegie’s classic guide will teach you how to stop catastrophizing and start enjoying life—without needing to move to a cabin in the woods.

Packed with tips to tame your inner worry-monster, it’s like therapy, but cheaper and with fewer awkward silences. Read it and finally give your overthinking brain the vacation it desperately needs!.
