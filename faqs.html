"Got questions?"

Perfect, because we’ve got a whole page of answers—some even make sense!
Got a stupid question?
No worries, we’ve got plenty of stupid answers to match. Dive in and let the chaos begin!

Do you really eat books?

Haha, absolutely not! We’re all about devouring books with our minds, not our mouths. Though, if you spot a few nibbled corners, it’s probably our pets getting curious. 😅

Can I join if I sometimes judge books by their covers?

Of course! We’d never judge—especially not when our shelves are arranged by cover color. We’re all about aesthetics and good vibes. ❤️📚

Can I bring my favorite book if it’s falling apart?

Absolutely! We consider cracked spines, dog-eared pages, and taped-up covers as proof of a book well-loved. Bring it along—it’s got character, just like you!

Do book moths read e-books?

Sure, we’re modern moths! Though occasionally, we might try to swipe a paperback like it’s a touchscreen. (Spoiler: It doesn’t work.)

Can I join if I’m a slow reader?

Absolutely! Whether you flutter through chapters or take your time savoring every word, we don’t speed-read shame. Think of yourself as a literary caterpillar—slow, steady, and destined to become a full-fledged Book Moth.

What happens if I accidentally fall asleep while reading?

Congratulations! You’ve unlocked the Ultimate Book Moth Achievement: drooling on a page while dreaming about the plot twist. Bonus points if you wake up and think it was part of the story!

What’s the club’s official motto?

“Eat, Read, Love.” But seriously, no eating books—we can’t stress this enough.
