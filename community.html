Our Flutter: A Community Like No Other!

Welcome to Our Flutter, where moth enthusiasts unite to celebrate all things fluttery and fabulous! We’re a quirky bunch of nature lovers, bookworms, and snack aficionados who have come together to share our passion for moths—and, of course, the art of snacking while doing it. You could say we’re like a cozy book club, but with a lot more winged friends and a bit less pretentiousness!"

Embrace the Community Spirit

What We Do

At Our Flutter, we eat, read, and discuss—often simultaneously! Our motto is simple: "Eat, Read, Discuss, Repeat!" Whether we’re munching on cookies or slurping up some tasty snacks, our meetings are fueled by delicious food and a shared love of literature

Explore new books

Buckle up, bookworms! Book Moth's Club is where reading gets wild—we turn page-turning into a contact sport of literary adventure. Expect mind-blowing books, snarky commentary, and the occasional dramatic reading that'll make your high school English teacher proud.

Join the Flutter!

So, if you’re ready to spread your wings and join a community that embraces the weird and wonderful, Our Flutter is the place for you. Bring your favorite snack, a book you can’t put down, and a sense of humor, and get ready to flutter into a world of friendship, laughter, and literary adventures.
